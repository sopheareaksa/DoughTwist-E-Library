Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about borrowing, accounts, and the library.

How do I borrow a book?

Find a book you love, open its detail page, and click the Borrow Now button. You can keep titles for up to 21 days, with one renewal if no one else has requested them.

Is DoughTwist free to use?

Yes. Creating an account, browsing the collection, and borrowing most titles are completely free. Some premium resources may require a membership upgrade.

Can I read books offline?

Many titles support offline reading through our web reader. Look for the Download option on supported book detail pages.

How do I contact support?

Visit our Help Center or send a message through the contact form. Our team typically responds within one business day.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help you make the most of the library. Reach out and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours.
